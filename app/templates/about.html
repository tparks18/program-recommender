About

Community gardens are vital in promoting civic engagement and fostering a sense of community. They provide a space for individuals to come together and work towards a common goal, such as growing fresh produce or beautifying the neighborhood. Through this shared experience, community members can develop a sense of ownership and pride in their community. Furthermore, community gardens can serve as an educational platform, a place for social interaction, and a means to build skills. They can provide access to fresh produce for those who may not have it otherwise. Lastly, they offer a platform for community members to come together and address issues that are important to them. Overall, community gardens are an effective way to promote community empowerment and civic engagement.


This inventory app lets community garden coordinators and volunteers easily keep track of garden plots, tools, and other resources, by streamlining the management process and providing a central location for information. The app aims to promote greater community engagement and the success of the community garden.
